What is This?

This is my public digital journal that I write in. It's purpose is 3 fold

  1. Replace my social media use with something a little less instant, and a little more personal
  2. Have a space to share my thoughts and find some therapy in writting about my life
  3. Share what I am working on and find some level of strange accountability as I put my progress into the ethernet.

I will be writing an entry every two weeks, which you can find by hitting the Most Recent post button
